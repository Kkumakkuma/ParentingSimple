### Common Installation Mistakes

Here are a few things to watch out for when installing your car seat:

1. **Incorrect Angle**: The seat should recline at the right angle, typically 45 degrees for newborns to prevent their head from falling forward.
2. **Base Not Secure**: The base should be installed firmly without any movement. Test it by grabbing the base and moving it side to side.
3. **Harness Mismanagement**: Be sure the harness straps are tight enough to prevent the child from sliding out but loose enough to easily place your baby in and out.
